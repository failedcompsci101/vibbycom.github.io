Frequently asked questions

About the company

What is Vibby? We live in a TL;DW (Too Long; Didn’t Watch) world and as more video is created, it becomes harder to find the moments that matter. Vibby is an interactive video platform that lets you highlight, share and discuss the best parts of any YouTube video. Our vision is to enable users to understand and utilize video in a more flexible, effective, and efficient way. Who is vibby for? Vibby is a free tool for anyone who wants to share video content in a more effective and efficient way. We work with individual creators and organizations alike that want to make the most of the video they share. Our users span many different industries and backgrounds, from education, to gaming, to sports, to music, to DIY videos, and many more. What is a vib? A vib is one or a collection of highlights made in a video. Think of it as your version of the video.

Using Vibby

Can I upload videos to Vibby? No. We only display videos already uploaded to YouTube and do not act as a video hosting service Can I track where my views are coming from? If you visit the dashboard you'll be able to see how all of your vibs are performing. How do I change my account page name? Click on yor username in upper right corner, then click on settings. You will be directed to the settings page where you will be able to change your username, password, email and profile picture..

Your Vib

Is there a way to search through videos on the vibby site? Yes. On vibby.com/explore you will see a search bar on the top left of the screen. There you can search by vib-title and by comments. In our next iterations, which will be release in a few weeks, you will be able to search for other users on the platform as well as search for Youtube videos directly on our site so you dont have to leave to get the link of a video you want to vib.

Your vib

Can I make my vib private? Yes, after creating your vib and hitting the next button, you will be taken to the next step, where you can name, save, and finish your vib. Next to the save button, you can check the “make this vib private” button. No one will be able to see your vib but you! Can I delete a vib? Yes. On your Dashboard you will see a red “Delete” button when you hover over the video thumbnail. Can I edit the length of a highlight in my vib? Move your cursor to either end of the highlight till you see the directional arrow show up. Then drag the desired length you’d like to change the highlight. Can I edit my vib after it was shared with someone? Yes you can! On your dashboard, when you hover over a vib thumbnail you’ll see two buttons show up, one to “Edit” you vib and the other to “Delete” it. Can I create a vib of a video on other sites like Vimeo or Facebook? At this time the only video streaming service we support is YouTube, but we will be adding more services in the near future. Can I delete a highlight on a vib? Not at this time, sorry! But, we are working on this, and you will see it really soon. Can other people comment on my vib? Anyone can comment on a vib, including both guests and registered users. In the near future, we plan to enable private vibs that can be shared only between you and friends.

Sharing & Embedding

How do I share my vib on social media? To share a video from the Vibby website, use the social links below the bottom right-hand corner of the video player How can I embed a Vibby video in my website? Visit https://www.vibby.com/embedding, enter a youtube or vibby link in input bar, select the size of the video you would like to embed, and then copy and paste the iframe script into your website. If you have any questions about embedding compatibility with specific content management systems and websites, email us at support@vibby.com. Stay tuned for advanced features for embedders to be released in the next few weeks. See: http://help.clip.mn/hc/en-us/articles/206471177-How-do-I-embed-ClipMine-player-on-my-WordPress-site- (its a good example)
